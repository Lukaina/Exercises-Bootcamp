Ada Lovelace

England, (1815-1852)

She was a mathematician, writer and computer scientist. Especially famous for his work on the general use calculator of Charles Babbage the so-called analytical machine.
Is considered as the first computer programmer.

Other occupations of Ada lovelace:

  • Developer
  • Engineer
  • Translator
NOTE: Each October, Ada Lovelace International Day is celebrated, a date that aims to commemorate the achievements of all women in technology, science, engineering or mathematics.
